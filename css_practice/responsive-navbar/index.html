About Us

Mangata and Gallo is a luxury jewelry store based in Austin, Texas, specializing in exquisite pieces for engagements, weddings, and anniversaries. Our jewelry, known for its high-quality craftsmanship and classic designs, reflects the essence of your special moments.

Founded and led by Mariana, an acclaimed jewelry designer with expertise in diamond cutting and metal smithing, our store offers a curated collection of exceptional pieces.
